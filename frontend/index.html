Blockchain Secure E-Voting System

Academic prototype — one voter, one vote. Votes stored on blockchain.

Welcome

Register to vote, then log in with your wallet to cast your vote. Results are stored immutably on the blockchain.

Register / Login Vote View Results

How it works

  • Register with your name, email, and MetaMask wallet.
  • Log in with the same wallet to vote.
  • Each wallet can vote only once (enforced by smart contract).
  • Votes are recorded on the blockchain and cannot be altered.
